If anyone wants to take a stab at the formula, go right ahead.
It seems a lot like the old formula at least for RK, SP is pretty linear though.
If I had to guess the missing numbers, I would say 18930hp for 138, and 21120hp for 146.
Those numbers are for trans jobs, keep in mind that they have 25% more HP than the normal base.
Normal values can be easily obtained by multiplying by 0.8 and rounding to the nearest whole.
Gravity tacks on the 25% after and floors the result.
